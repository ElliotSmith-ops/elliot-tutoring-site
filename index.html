About Me

Hi! I’m Elliot — a USC graduate in Computer Science & Business Administration, and a lifelong educator with a passion for helping students feel excited about learning again.

Before tutoring full-time, I ran one of Seattle’s most popular summer camps as Youth Programs Director at the Center for Wooden Boats. I’ve led month-long teen leadership expeditions, coached thousands of students, and built programs that kids actually look forward to.

Some of the best compliments I’ve received? That I bring the curiosity of Carl Sagan and the enthusiasm of Steve Irwin into every session. Whether we’re working on executive function, SAT prep, or writing skills, I blend structure with empathy to help students become more confident, focused, and independent — while genuinely enjoying the process.
